# Centle CRM/ERP System 🚀

Welcome to the **Centle CRM/ERP**! Imagine a huge company that actually runs six smaller, totally different businesses (like a tech team, an events team, and a marketing team). Usually, each of these small businesses would use their own separate apps to keep track of their customers and money. This creates a giant mess because the main boss can't see everything in one place.

Centle CRM/ERP is the "super app" that fixes this problem! It brings all six businesses together into one massive, smart system. 

## 🌟 Coolest Features

1. **The Venture Switcher:** With one click, you can switch the entire app to look at just one specific business (we call them "ventures"), or zoom out and look at all six combined!
2. **AI Assistant:** It has a built-in robot brain! If you have a customer who hasn't replied in a week, the AI will notice, give them a "Low Health" score, and even write a custom email for you to send them to win them back.
3. **The Ambassador War Room:** You can give special links to your friends (Ambassadors). If they get someone to buy something using their link, the system automatically tracks it and pays your friend a commission! 
4. **Drag-and-Drop Pipeline:** You can organize all your potential deals on a giant digital whiteboard and just drag them across the screen when you close a sale.
5. **Completely Mobile Friendly:** The whole app works perfectly on your laptop, iPad, or smartphone!

## 👥 Who Uses This?

- **The Boss (Admins):** Gets to see the giant Dashboard showing how much money all six businesses are making.
- **The Team Leaders (Managers):** They filter the app so they only see the people and money for *their* specific business.
- **The Sales Team (Reps):** The people making the calls, sending the emails, and closing the deals using the AI assistant to help them work faster.
- **The Referrers (Ambassadors):** Regular people who just log in to see how much money they've made from sharing their referral links.

## 🛠️ How It Was Built
- **Next.js & React:** The code that makes the website look and act like a super-fast app.
- **Tailwind CSS:** What makes it look so cool, dark, and futuristic (with lots of blurry glass effects!).
- **Supabase:** The giant filing cabinet (database) in the cloud where all the customer data is safely locked away.

This is a really advanced system that turns a chaotic group of businesses into one smooth, organized machine!
